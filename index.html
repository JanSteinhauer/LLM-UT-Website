About Us

Welcome to the Large Language Model Organization at the University of Texas at Austin! We are a community dedicated to exploring and advancing the field of large language models (LLMs). Our mission is to foster collaboration, share knowledge, and inspire innovation in the realm of artificial intelligence and natural language processing.

Join us for the kickoff meeting to learn more about the Large Language Model Organization at UT Austin. This meeting will cover our mission, upcoming projects, and how you can get involved. LLM Timeline Overview

Explore the development timeline of Large Language Models (LLMs) from their inception to the present day. This session will cover key milestones in LLM development, including notable models like GPT, BERT, and newer innovations. Understand how these advancements have shaped the field of AI and what the future holds for large-scale language processing.

Prompt Tuning Workshop

Learn how to fine-tune prompts to get the best results from LLMs in this hands-on workshop. We'll dive into techniques for crafting effective prompts that can optimize model outputs for a variety of tasks. Whether you're working on text generation, summarization, or any other application, this workshop will equip you with the skills to maximize your LLM's performance.

Feature-Based vs. Finetuning

Understand the difference between feature-based approaches and fine-tuning in model training. This session will compare these two techniques, discussing when to use each approach based on your specific needs. We'll cover case studies, best practices, and the trade-offs involved in choosing between feature extraction and full model fine-tuning.

Benefits of Joining LLM Organization

Neat Community

Become part of a vibrant community of AI enthusiasts, researchers, and professionals who share a passion for large language models. Engage in collaborative projects, discussions, and activities that foster growth and learning.

Engaging Events

Attend a variety of events such as workshops, seminars, and talks by industry experts. These events are designed to keep you updated with the latest trends in AI and LLMs while providing hands-on learning opportunities.

Interesting Speakers

Learn from and interact with distinguished speakers from the field of artificial intelligence. Our guest speakers include top researchers, industry leaders, and innovators who offer unique insights into the world of LLMs.

Join Us

Are you passionate about AI and LLMs? Join our organization and become part of a community that is shaping the future of language models. Whether you're a seasoned expert or just starting out, there's a place for you in our community.
